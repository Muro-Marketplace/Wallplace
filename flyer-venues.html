Wallspace How It Works

A Curated Marketplace · Two Sides · Zero Risk

One platform. Built for both sides.

Wallspace connects photographers and artists who want commercial exposure with venues that want original art — with flexible, risk-free arrangements.

For Artists

01

Apply

Submit your portfolio for review. We respond within 5 business days. No AI art.

02

Get Curated

Your profile goes live. Venues browse, filter, and reach out to you directly.

03

Earn

Sell prints outright, offer bundles, take commissions, or arrange revenue-share deals.

For Venues

01

Browse & Filter

Search curated portfolios by location, style, and theme. Completely free to browse.

02

Enquire Directly

Message artists through the platform. Discuss your space and agree terms.

03

Get Art on Your Walls

Arrange free loan, revenue share, or direct purchase. Swap or rotate any time.

Flexible Arrangements

Free Loan

Display artwork at no cost. Return or rotate any time. Zero risk.

Revenue Share

Customer buys art from your wall — you earn a share. No cost to the venue.

Direct Purchase

Buy artwork outright for your permanent collection at fair, transparent prices.

Pricing at a Glance

Artists

First 20 artists: free for life

Core plans otherwise start from £9.99/month. 0–10% platform fee on sales.

Venues

Always free to browse & enquire

Zero upfront cost. Premium features available. You share in the sale, not the cost.
